About NeoLog

NeoLog is a premium publication covering the intersection of artificial intelligence, science fiction, cinema, and original storytelling. We publish daily content that informs, entertains, and inspires — all generated with the assistance of cutting-edge AI tools and human editorial oversight.

Our Mission

To make the future accessible. We believe that AI, sci-fi, and technology should not be the exclusive domain of experts. Our content translates complex ideas into engaging stories that anyone can understand and enjoy.

Our Topics

🤖 AI & Tech — Latest AI developments, tool reviews, and practical guides.
🚀 Sci-Fi — Movie reviews, book analysis, and genre commentary.
🎬 Movies — Honest film reviews and industry analysis.
✍️ Stories — Original fiction exploring futuristic themes.

Our Stack

NeoLog runs on a fully local, zero-cost infrastructure: Hermes Agent for automation, Ollama for local AI inference, GitHub Pages for hosting, and a MacBook M4 Pro — all running on-device with no cloud dependency.

Transparency

We use AI tools to assist in research and drafting. All content is reviewed, edited, and approved by a human editor before publication. Where affiliate links are used, they are clearly disclosed in accordance with ASCI Guidelines 2026 and applicable laws.
